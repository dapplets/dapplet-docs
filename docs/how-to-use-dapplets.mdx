To use a dapplets, follow these steps.

#### 1. Open the extension.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_01.png)

#### 2. Connect wallet.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_02.png)

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_03.png)

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_04.png)

#### 3. Activate the dapplet you need.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_05.png)

Wait for the switch to activate.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_06.png)

:::tip

Some dapplets require a page reload to use.

:::

#### 4. If the dapplet has a house icon click on it to use the interface.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_14.png)

Some dapplets require a login to use the functions.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_17.png)

You can go to the settings of the dapplet from the overlay interface...

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_18.png)

... or from home page.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_15.png)

Also on the home page of the extension, you can go to the dapplet page in the ** Dapplet's store **.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_16.png)

Page's dapplet in **Dapplet`s store**.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_19.png)

#### 5. In the **Dapplet`s store** you can add a dapplet to your lists.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_20.png)

:::tip

**Public list**

Add dapplets here for public use and for share it to you followers.

:::

:::tip

**Local list**

Add dapplets here for local use from dapplets card.

:::

:::tip

**Trusted user**

Listers whose dapplets you want to use.

:::

#### 6. Here you can filter the list of dapplets in the extension

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_13.png)



#### 7. To display dapplets in your lists, you need to add **Trusted user** in the extension settings.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_07.png)

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_09.png)

:::tip

In the **Settings** there are already automatically added trusted users. click on the menu arrow to view.

:::

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_08.png)

#### 8. On the **homepage** you can find the right dapplet.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_10.png)

#### 9. Also go to **Dapplet's store**.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_11.png)

#### 10. Using the shared state button, you can share the version of the extension and the configuration saved in it.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_12.png)

Click the button. Wait for the copy icon to appear.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_21.png)

Paste the copied link into the search bar and hit enter.

![Dapplets List of Extension](/img/use_22.png)

Your extension configuration will start downloading automatically.
